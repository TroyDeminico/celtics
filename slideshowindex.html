Championship Pedigree

The Celtics have won a total of 17 championships, tying them with the Lakers for the most in NBA history. Boston's most recent title came in 2008 when it defeated the Lakers in six games. The Celtics are 17-5 in the NBA Finals. The Boston Celtics won the NBA Finals 17 times, in 1957, 1959, 1960, 1961, 1962, 1963, 1964, 1965, 1966, 1968, 1969, 1974, 1976, 1981, 1984, 1986 and 2008.
