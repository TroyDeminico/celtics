The team’s entire rotation outside of Grant Williams is already under contract for next season. If Jaylen Brown becomes eligible for a supermax contract, it’s possible the team’s top seven player will be signed through the 2024-25 season as soon as this summer with Al Horford being the only player in the that group over age 31. The focus is understandable on the present at the moment for the Celtics who have a tremendous opportunity to get back to the NBA Finals in back-to-back seasons for the first time since the 1980s. If they can achieve that, the future will only grow brighter for this core as age and finances catch up with other contending cores in the Eastern Conference.
